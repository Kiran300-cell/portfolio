Project Case Study

Omni-Directional Smart Vehicle

An advanced robotic vehicle capable of 90-degree wheel rotation, enabling multi-directional movement and improved navigation in confined environments.

Problem

Traditional robotic vehicles are limited to forward, backward, and turning motions, which makes precise movement difficult in tight or complex environments.

Solution

I designed an omni-directional smart vehicle in which all four wheels can rotate up to 90 degrees, allowing sideways motion, diagonal movement, and rotation in place.

Highlights

  • Designed a wheel mechanism enabling 90-degree rotation.
  • Supported multi-directional movement and improved mobility.
  • Implemented wireless and microcontroller-based control.
  • Focused on applications where maneuverability matters.

Stack

Microcontroller-based control, Embedded C/C++, motor drivers, wireless communication, mechanical design, and real-time coordination logic.
